New York Times Comedy Corpus (NYTCC)

In 2023, I created the NYTCC, a 7.68-million-word corpus, consisting of thousands of NYT articles about comedy written between 2003 and 2023. I used this corpus to analyze changes in media infrastructures and discourse surrounding comedy over time. If you would like to use the NYTCC, feel free to shoot me an email!
