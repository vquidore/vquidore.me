Mission Statement

I believe in the power and potential of public media, and I am passionate about repairing America's media and information ecosystems. And while some day, I hope to ultimately contribute to that change on a large scale, infrasructural level, for now, I am trying to build community in all that I do from as many different perspectives as possible, whether it be tinkering and telling stories in radio stations, designing games that spark curiosity, or coding art installations that offer new perspectives on togetherness and memory.

This mission, like me, will inevitably change, but for now,

we're here.
